Arcade Tools

Cool tools for the Hack Club Arcade! By Hayden Kong.

The Arcade Journal

Write thank-you notes to the team of staff and volunteers who made Hack Club Arcade possible! Collectely we can make a huge thank-you card!!

The Arcade Tracker

Input your ticket goal, how many tickets you currently have, plus how many hours you can work and it will automatically calculate how many hours you need to complete per day to reach your goal! It also provides useful insights such as a progress bar showing your progress towards your goal.

The Arcade Countdown

The countdown until Arcade is over! *with live chat!

Arcade AI-powered Idea Generator

Stuck with what project you want to make? This idea-generator can give you great ideas based on your interests!

Arcade Review Stats

View the latest review stats, as well as a graph that updates every 5 minutes to see how the backlog is doing! (its decreasing, hopefully)

How it works

  1. Work on projects - Hack on something cool! Try this AI-powered idea generator if you need some help!
  2. Bank your hours - Join the Hack Club Slack and use /hack in #arcade to log your hours! Wondering how long it might take? Check out the live Arcade Review Stats!
  3. Redeem your Prizes - Use your tickets to buy prizes for your next project! Check out the shop!
